End-to-End Development for Global Brands.

We conceptualise beautiful, effective, sustainable brands.
Then we develop, launch & distribute them into the market.

Product Conceptualization

We are constantly researching the market to see what is new on top of product trends, meaning we pass this expert knowledge onto our customers. You don't need to be the product experts, let us be!

Designing

We have a fully fledged in-house design studio, who can carry out all types 2D and 3D design requirements. They have over 20 years of experience in working across the full range of product categories, allowing them to come up with innovative and striking product designs. They also have the capability to produce pre-production samples.

Production

Our 2 fully owned manufacturing units allow us to be very flexible, while also allowing us to deliver high quality outputs. We will only release a product once we are 100% certain it matches the very highest of Quality Control checks. We even have in-house vacuum forming, blow moulding, metalising, lacquering and Screen Printing facilities.

Vertically Integrated

From the Product Conceptualization stage, through to the Design stage, through to the Production stage, through to the Quality Control stage, through to our value added services and distribution network, we can truly say we are a global integrated company with many functions such as bulk development, fragrance development, Vacuum Forming and international distribution capabilities.

Quality Control

Having well developed in-house quality control departments allows us to keep stringent checks on all batches of incoming components and outgoing products, all in line with military standards.
