Our Story

We design, develop, produce and market a wide range of vibrant and innovative products in the realms of (but not restricted to) Fragrances, Deodorants, Colour Cosmetics, Beauty and Personal Care.

We have 2 sides of our business:

  • OWN BRAND Portfolio: We manufacture and market a wide range of our OWN Brands across a range of market demographics.
  • PRIVATE LABEL Division: We take on select turn-key projects, providing an end-to-end integrated service to global brands such as Freeman Beauty, Parfume De Couer, Body Fantasies, Bod, Cristiano Ronaldo, Yardley, Fogg, Enchanetuer, Delta Goodrem.
