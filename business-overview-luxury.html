Business Overview

Excellence in every endeavor.

A Dual Approach to Excellence

We have TWO sides to our business. One is our OWN LABEL division, while the other is our PRIVATE LABEL division.

Under these we develop brands and products across our FOUR main product parallels: Fragrances, Personal Care & Beauty, Colour Cosmetics & Deodorants.

40+ Years of Trust

Own Brands

We Design, Develop, Manufacture and Distribute a wide range of fully owned brands across the Fragrance, Personal Care & Beauty, Deodorants, and Colour Cosmetics sectors. Some of our brands have been present in the market for more than 40 years, sold in over 70 countries globally.
