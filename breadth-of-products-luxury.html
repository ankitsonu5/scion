Breadth of Products

Our aim is to deliver the evocative pleasures of Fragrance & Beauty products to each and every persons life.

We do this via 15 different brands, across the Fragrance, Personal Care & Beauty, Deodorants and Colour Cosmetics categories.

Our vast expertise, largely in-house manufacturing setup and vast industry experience allow us to produce and distribute a huge range of premium Luxury, Masstige, Mass Market, and Value products. We produce about 1500 different stock keeping units (SKU's).
