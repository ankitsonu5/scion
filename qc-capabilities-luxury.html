Quality Assurance

QC Capabilities

Controlling Quality from raw material stage right through to on-shelf.

The deep level of vertical integration that we have has given us the ability to control Quality from the raw material stage, right through to on-shelf.

Stringent Controls

Building upon years of experience, Scion has also developed an integrated quality assurance approach that focuses on high standards. We adhere to military standards of quality control and check all batches of incoming components and outgoing finished goods.

Dedicated Resources

We truly pride ourselves on the high level of quality we can provide our customers, so continued investment means we have a dedicated QC department in each of our manufacturing units, whose sole responsibility is to ensure our products match our high standards of quality.

Accreditations

Our constant and dedicated focus on Quality Control throughout our organization processes has led to us being awarded numerous international accreditations; to name a few are ISO 9001, ISO 22716, ISO 14001 and more. All our manufacturing plants are also accredited with standards.

Excellence In Analysis

Quality Control Laboratories

Our Quality Control laboratories are equipped with a wide range of constantly updated equipment, allowing us to ensure that both incoming and outgoing products adhere to our high level of Quality Control standards.
