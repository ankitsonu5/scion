Our Pillars of Excellence

Business Strengths

The foundations that power our global legacy in luxury perfumery.

Committed to Perfection

We are committed to delivering the best in class Products & Services to our customers each day. Our strength lies in our comprehensive capabilities and global reach.

Widespread Global Presence

We truly have global presence with our brands sold in over 70 countries distributing through a wide retail and wholesale sales network with offices in 7 countries.

Highly Vertically Integrated

From product conceptualization, design, production, to sales & distribution network; we can truly say we are a globally integrated company.

World Class R&D Function

We do all our Research & development in house, through 2 highly developed R&D and Innovation centers. This allows us to be firmly on top of quality.

Diversity & Experience

We employ over 1000 people worldwide, from more than 30 different nationalities across 7 different global locations. A truly diverse workforce.

Huge Range of Products

We design and develop a huge range of products within fragrances, colour cosmetics & personal care. From 5ml to 500ml, our flexibility is unmatched.

Global Supply Chain

Distribution offices across 8 countries. Our brands are sold in beauty stores, drug stores, fashion stores, pharmacies, mass merchandisers, and e-commerce.

45 Years Experience

In this industry since 1974, we have grown to become of one the largest manufacturing companies offering a wide range of product categories.

Highly Developed Manufacturing

Brand new, fully customized manufacturing plant in India with an incredibly high level of vertical integration and state-of-the-art machinery.

Catering to Global Giants

We cater to some of the biggest retailers in the world including Wal Mart, Tesco, Carrefour, Walgreens, Target, and perform Private Label for major brands.

Creating evocative pleasures of Fragrance & Beauty worldwide since 1974.
