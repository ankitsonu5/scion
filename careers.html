Join the Scion Family


This is a call to all our prospective employees who can look forward to successful careers and rewarding potential here with us. Scion offers a challenging and creative career path for those who want to realize their full potential. A career at Scion International is a path to personal and professional growth, integrity and human connection.

Reasons to Work with Us

Global Interest

Being a multinational company we can offer you international exposure & great potential for growth globally.

Direct Access & Learning From Management

We have a very open policy, where every single level of management is directly accessible to all. We believe in transparency & open discussions.

Freedom to Grow

As we are a large family business we value organic growth. Those who bring promise, hard work & dedication will see their careers bloom.

Happiness

Total satisfaction working in a positive, stimulating and ethical working environment. Our environment is like a second home.

Wide Exposure

Because we encourage personal growth, we allow individuals across departments to expose themselves to different areas of our business.

Work - Life Balance

Safe and purely professional environment which strictly adheres to labor laws and respects personal time.

Benefits

We ensure that our employees are well taken care of via competitive compensation packages and benefits.

Diverse & Inclusive Environment

We are a multi-cultural organization and we respect different cultures and individuals. We are an equal opportunity employer.

People Development

As we are growing fast, we are continuously providing training and support to our employees, helping them adapt and learn.
